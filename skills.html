Required Skills

Creating content isn't for everyone, which is pretty self-explanatory,
though if you do want to give it a go, their are a few unspoken needed skills,
editing, organization and casting! Those are three of the most important assets,
or at least they are for someone who makes my type of content ~

Organization

Organization is just as keen, it's less about managing the video itself and more about helping you get it out during a certain dead-line. Typically I organize my videos before and while I make them, utilizing three things;

    - Recording NPC voice lines.
    - Swapping out and around scenes.
    - Distributing audio and video.


Casting

Casting is equally as important. It's used for finding voices for other characters, if thats the type of your content your creating that is, which is why I have three different methods for finding voice actors;

    - Public casting calls on discord.
    - Tag in one of your friends.
    - look for likeminded creators.
